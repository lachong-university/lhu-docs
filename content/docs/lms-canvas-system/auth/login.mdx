# How to Login to Moodle LMS

## Prerequisites

- Valid user account credentials (username/email and password)
- Access to the Moodle site URL

## Login Steps

1. **Navigate to the Moodle site**

- Open your web browser
- Enter the Moodle URL provided by your institution

2. **Locate the login form**

- Look for the "Log in" link (usually in the top-right corner)
- Click on it to access the login page

3. **Enter your credentials**

- **Username/Email**: Enter your assigned username or email address
- **Password**: Enter your password (case-sensitive)

4. **Login options**

- Check "Remember username" to save your username for future logins
- Click the "Log in" button

## Alternative Login Methods

### Single Sign-On (SSO)

If your institution uses SSO:

- Click on the SSO provider button (e.g., "Login with Google", "Login with Microsoft")
- Follow the authentication process

### Guest Access

Some courses may allow guest access:

- Look for "Log in as a guest" button
- Click to enter without credentials (limited functionality)

## Troubleshooting

### Forgot Password

1. Click "Forgotten your username or password?" link
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions

### Account Locked

- Contact your system administrator
- Wait for the lockout period to expire (if applicable)

### Browser Issues

- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Disable browser extensions temporarily

## First Time Login

- You may be prompted to change your password
- Complete any required profile information
- Accept terms and conditions if presented
